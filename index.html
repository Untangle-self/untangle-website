Untangle

From confusion to clarity

Untangle is an emotional clarity system that helps you slow down, name what you are feeling, and find grounded next steps. It turns mental noise into a clear path you can actually trust.

What it is

A calm reflection space designed to help you move through overwhelm with structure. Untangle combines gentle prompts and practical framing so you can understand what is happening inside, instead of getting stuck in loops.

How it works

  1. Pause and capture what feels heavy or unclear right now.
  2. Separate facts, feelings, and needs to reveal what matters most.
  3. Choose one simple next action that brings immediate emotional relief.
